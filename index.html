Vanilla Bean Macarons

These babys are classic for a reason - timeless, simple, elegant and bursting with rich, creamy vanilla flavor. The delicate almond shells are speckled with real vanilla bean seeds and pair perfectlly with a super smooth vanilla bean buttercream filling. Whether you are a macaron newbie or a seasoned baker, mastering this flavor is a must. It's the little black dress of macarons - always in style and everyone needs one.

Blue Raspberry Macarons

Blue Raspberry Macarons are a bold twist on this classic French treat. Their vibrant blue hue and the tangy-sweet raspberry filling hits that fruity spot everytime. The tartness of the raspberry balances the sweetness of the shells, creating a playful flavor combo that's hard to resist. If you are looking to add a pop of color and burst of fruitiness to your macaron lineup, these blue beauties are it.
